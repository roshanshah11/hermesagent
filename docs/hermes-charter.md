## Part A — Latent obligations Roshan is probably NOT tracking

Things that *might* belong on the list given his situation (UChicago transfer, sophomore, IB/quant target, Oct 1 recruiting ramp, startup, $100/day, family business) but aren't visible in the 213 rows. **Register: confirm, don't assume** — some may already be handled (credits processed, family covers housing). His list says "Recruiting ramp → Oct 1" as one row — that's not a system. **Hermes should maintain a live recruiting calendar: which firm opens when, deadline, application surface, status.** A miss here can't be recovered.

3. **Resume + answer library.** He'll write "why this program / why finance / walk me through your background" twenty times across the UChicago Financial Markets app, Contrary, clubs, and recruiting. **Hermes maintains a polished resume and a reusable answer bank** to remix — instead of starting cold each time.

4. **A running accomplishments / "brag doc."** Transfers and recruits forget what they did. **Hermes logs his wins as they happen** (from his activity) so resume/interview/application prep is never archaeology.

5. **$100/day funnel build (post-decision).** Once he picks a channel/offering, there's real setup he hasn't scoped: landing page or booking link, payment (Stripe), platform profile, and **outbound lead-gen to prospective clients** — all agent-ownable except the pricing decision.

6. **Family business — named as a goal, absent from the board.** Honest split: relationships/strategy/family dynamics are his; **the agent-ownable slices (market & competitor research, a recurring "what's moving in our market" digest, process/data cleanup) are real and unclaimed.** Worth deciding if any enters the system at all.

7. **Capacity / deadline-collision flags.** He's running internship + summer classes + placement + recruiting + startup + Substack + $100/day + family + tabla/golf/gym. **Hermes can flag the week where deadlines pile up** before it hits him (feeds the reflow — doesn't replace it).

## Part B — New capability ideas (beyond his current list)

8. **Pre-meeting briefs — likely the single best networking feature.** Before any call/coffee chat, a one-pager on the person: background, recent work/posts, mutual connections, smart talking points, the ask. Turns every conversation into a prepared one. Pure agent work.

9. **Professor & lab research monitoring.** Track his target profs' publications/projects (dept pages, arXiv/SSRN). When one ships something, Hermes pings him with a *hook* to reach out — so outreach is timely and specific, not generic. Directly serves the research-at-UChicago goal, recurring.

10. **Warm-intro path finder.** Given a target person/firm, surface the warm path through his network (who he knows who knows them). Built on the contact CRM.

11. **News/event triggers on his network.** "X got promoted / posted / raised / moved firms — good moment to reach out." Converts a static contact list into live opportunities.

12. **Inbound email triage + draft replies** (if he grants email access). Triage to skip/info/action, draft the action ones in his voice. Chief-of-staff pattern. *(Optional — bigger access ask.)*

13. **AI/tech reading digest** (companion to the markets digest) — keeps him current for the tech side of his finance/tech identity and startup work.

## Part C — Final consolidated charter — what Hermes should be able to do

Five pillars. Each line is agent-ownable today (research/draft/build/monitor); the decision/send/relationship stays human.

### 1. Research & Intelligence (core engine)
- **Company/stock deep-dives** → dossier + first-pass DCF/EV model + draft thesis for Substack *(he picks the company; he writes the take)*.
- **Markets digest + portfolio/watchlist tracking** → daily, annotated, with events on his holdings.
- **On-demand topical research** → $100/day market, HF-vs-IB requirements, startup idea validation, family-business market scans.
- **Professor & lab research monitoring** → track target profs' output, surface outreach hooks.
- **Research knowledge base** → everything Hermes finds, filed and searchable. Never re-research.

### 2. Networking & Outreach (first-class pillar — his priority)
- **Contact CRM** → living People DB: last touch, next action, context, warm-path.
- **Pre-meeting briefs** → one-pager on anyone before a call.
- **Outreach drafting** → cold emails, DMs, follow-ups in his voice (Daniel/Youti/Yoyo and beyond). *He sends.*
- **Follow-up engine** → who's gone cold, who owes a reply, news/promotion triggers.
- **Warm-intro path finder + alumni mapping** (UChicago → finance/tech/quant).

### 3. Production & Build
- **Internship deliverables** → broker-listing scraper + industry research, run on a schedule (the unattended counterpart to him driving Claude Code live).
- **Trading-systems workstream** *(raised in a prior session — confirm it's still live)* → data pulls, signal research, backtests, walk-forward validation, strategy journal. If still a goal, it's the cleanest agent domain he has.
- **Personal coding (Remote Connect)** → per-feature, on a branch for review.
- **Substack distribution** → repurpose each deep-dive into X/LinkedIn.

### 4. Logistics & Deadline Radar (the stuff that silently expires)
- **UChicago transfer onboarding tracker** → registration, transfer-credit, advising, housing, health forms, orientation.
- **Recruiting deadline radar** → IB/quant sophomore timelines, diversity programs, applications, status.
- **Application drafting + answer library** → program/fellowship/club apps from his background; reusable answer bank.
- **Resume + accomplishments log** → kept current automatically.
- **Travel & logistics research** → SF housing/flights (Startup School Jul 24–26) and future trips.
- **$100/day funnel build** → landing/booking/payment/lead-gen once the channel is chosen.

### 5. Operating Cadence (always-on heartbeat)
- **Morning brief** → today's schedule + the 1–3 things that matter + deadline proximity.
- **Due-tomorrow pre-load** → tomorrow's research/materials staged the night before.
- **Weekly-review prep** → reconcile slips, pre-draft next week's research queue, refresh cold-contacts.
- **Master deadline monitor** → one radar across academics, recruiting, applications, content — feeds the reflow, never silently drops.
